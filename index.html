How to Play

In Valorant Speed Typer, you'll race against the clock to type as many words as possible.

The game will display a random word, and you'll need to type it accurately before the time runs out.

Earn points for each word you type correctly, and try to beat your previous high score!

You can choose from three difficulty levels: Easy, Medium, and Hard. The higher the difficulty, the less time you'll get for each correct word typed.

On the Easy level, you'll get 7 seconds added for each correct word. On Medium, you'll get 4 seconds added. And on Hard, you'll get 2 seconds added.
